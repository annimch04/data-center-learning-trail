# Missouri Site-Selection Prompts

## Rubric Design

```text
Help me improve a public-facing data center site-selection rubric for Missouri. The rubric compares site archetypes, not exact parcels. Evaluate whether the categories are complete, whether any category overlaps too much with another, and what evidence would be needed before assigning scores.
```

## Power And Transmission

```text
Explain how to evaluate power availability and transmission constraints for a proposed large-load data center site in Missouri. Separate what can be learned from public documents from what would require utility or operator conversations.
```

## Public Benefit

```text
Act as a neutral public policy reviewer. What would make a data center project a strong public benefit case for a rural Missouri community, and what risks should be evaluated before incentives are offered?
```

## Field Interview Prep

```text
Help me prepare interview questions for a local official, utility representative, broadband stakeholder, or economic development leader about data center site feasibility. The goal is to understand constraints and public benefit, not to advocate for or against a specific project.
```

## Memo Review

```text
Review this memo for unsupported claims, missing evidence, and unclear reasoning. Flag any statements that should be framed as hypotheses instead of conclusions.
```
