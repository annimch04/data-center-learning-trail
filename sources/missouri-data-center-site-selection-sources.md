# Missouri Site-Selection Source Log

Created: 2026-06-06

This source log supports the first draft of the Missouri Data Center Site-Selection Rubric.

## Starting Sources

### Missouri Data Centers And Permitting

- Missouri Department of Natural Resources, "Data Centers"  
  https://dnr.mo.gov/data-e-services/centers  
  Notes: Starting point for state-level data center context, DNR permitting touchpoints, energy/water/air considerations, and Missouri's current review of energy regulations and infrastructure planning around AI data centers.

### State Incentives

- Missouri Department of Economic Development, "Data Center Sales Tax Exemption Program"  
  https://ded.mo.gov/programs/business/data-center-sales-tax-exemption-program  
  Notes: Starting point for understanding Missouri's data center tax incentive structure.

### Broadband And Rural Connectivity

- Missouri Department of Economic Development, "Broadband Equity, Access, and Deployment (BEAD) Program"  
  https://ded.mo.gov/programs/community/broadband-equity-access-and-deployment-bead-program  
  Notes: Program page for broadband deployment funding to unserved and underserved areas and community anchor institutions.

- Missouri Department of Economic Development, "Office of Broadband Broadband Availability Mapping"  
  https://ded.mo.gov/office-broadband-development/challenge  
  Notes: Describes the Missouri Broadband Map and classifications such as served, underserved, unserved, BEAD eligible, and non-BEAD eligible.

- Missouri Public Service Commission, "Broadband"  
  https://psc.mo.gov/Telecommunications/Broadband  
  Notes: Links to broadband mapping and related Missouri broadband resources.

### Underground Infrastructure And Existing Data Center Assets

- Bluebird Fiber, "Data Center Services & Colocation"  
  https://bluebirdfiber.com/data-center/  
  Notes: Bluebird describes its Springfield, Missouri underground data center and connectivity services.

- The Center for Land Use Interpretation, "Springfield Underground"  
  https://clui.org/projects/hollowed-earth/underground-storage-and-business-parks-former-limestone-mines/springfield  
  Notes: Useful background on Springfield Underground as a former limestone mine and underground business park; should be paired with operator or official sources for current claims.

### Power And Transmission Planning

- Ameren, "Integrated Resource Plan"  
  https://www.ameren.com/sustainability/policy/IRP  
  Notes: Entry point for Ameren Missouri planning materials, including load forecasting, planning environment, and transmission/distribution chapters.

- MISO, "MTEP"  
  https://www.misoenergy.org/planning/  
  Notes: MISO's annual transmission expansion planning process.

- Southwest Power Pool, "Transmission Planning"  
  https://www.spp.org/engineering/transmission-planning/  
  Notes: SPP planning models and transmission planning process; relevant because Missouri sits near/within multiple transmission planning contexts.

## Source Rules For This Project

- Prefer official public sources, operator pages, regulatory filings, planning documents, public maps, and local government records.
- Use media and commentary to identify questions, not as final proof.
- Capture access dates for source-sensitive claims.
- Separate facts, interpretations, and hypotheses.
- Do not include non-public documents, confidential information, or private conversations unless explicitly cleared for public use.
